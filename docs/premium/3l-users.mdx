# 3 Letter Usernames

With [emogir.ls Premium](https://emogir.ls/pricing), you unlock access to `3 letter usernames`, which gives you a shorter username that can make your account more unique.

To get a 3 letter username:
- Buy [emogir.ls Premium](https://emogir.ls/pricing) 
- Head to [Settings](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/settings) 
- Under **`General Information`**, click on **`Username`**. 
- Change to a 3 letter username
- Click on `Save Changes`
