# Free Custom Badge

With [emogir.ls Premium](https://emogir.ls/pricing), you can create your own free custom badge.

---

## What's a custom badge?
- A custom badge is a badge you can fully customize to how you desire. You can upload any image you want, and even give the badge its own name that will show on your profile!

### How do I claim my custom badge?
To claim your free custom badge:
- Head over to **[Account â†’ Badges](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/badges)**
- Under **`Custom Badges`**, click on **`Create Custom Badge`**
- Enter a ame for your badge & an icon for your badge
- Click `Create Badge`
- Click on `Save Changes`

Now, when you head over to your profile, you'll find your own custom badge!

<Admonition type="info" title="Information">
**You can toggle your badge on/off at any time by clicking on it in the [Badges Tab](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/badges).**
</Admonition>
